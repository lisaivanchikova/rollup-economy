NAICS structure

Per US Census 2022 NAICS, 4-digit 5415 contains 541511 Custom Computer Programming Services, 541512 Computer Systems Design, 541513 Computer Facilities Management, 541519 Other Computer Related Services. All four share the Hell-Yes drivers. 541511 is where AI-native services studios (fractional CTO, AI engineering pods, custom AI agent development on platforms like n8n/LangChain) operate.

Counter-thesis — what would weaken the sub-vertical

Threats to monitor

  • Competitive thesis crowding — operator-led AI services consolidation already articulated by Pioneers/Fonzi/Chassi/Cyborg HR; finite pool of senior AI engineering boutiques
  • SR&ED policy risk for Canadian operators — 2024 federal budget reviews considered narrowing CCPC enhanced refundability; structural moat depends on single legislative program
  • Big consulting (Accenture, Deloitte, Infosys) move down-market into mid-market AI services; would compress entry multiples and talent rates
  • Customer concentration in venture-backed startups creates cyclicality if funding markets tighten

Cross-vertical claim caveat (not part of this decision)

  • The "5415 operator acquires 5418 marketing agencies" thesis is a separate two-vertical decision, not a 541511 sub-vertical claim
  • 5418 Advertising scores Conditional on main map (ΣS=20,916) — in active AI disruption with multiples compressing 6-9× → 3-5×
  • Combined value-creation math (5-8× per tuck-in) is plausible but not the sub-vertical decision being made here
  • This 541511 decision stands on the AI engineering services merit alone, not on the cross-vertical play

What would have to be true to invest

  • Operator with audited financials demonstrating 30%+ EBITDA on AI engineering services book (above the 5415 aggregate 20.5%)
  • Pipeline of 3-5 boutique 541511 targets at $2-10M revenue with 20%+ EBITDA and demonstrated AI delivery (not just AI marketing)
  • Canadian operator with verified SR&ED claim history (actual refunds received, not modeled) — structural moat is real if claims are filed and approved
  • Customer concentration discipline — not >60% from venture-backed startups (cyclical demand)
  • Talent retention through ownership change — senior engineers can walk; equity rollover required
